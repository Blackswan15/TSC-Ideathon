CREATE A POST

Share your thoughts, spark a discussion, or post a complaint anonymously on the main feed for the community to see.

ENGAGE AND VOTE

Interact with content by upvoting or downvoting posts and complaints. Join the conversation by commenting under any post.

BUILD CREDIBILITY

Gain or lose credential points based on community feedback. Posts with high upvotes earn you +30 points, while heavily downvoted posts will cost you -30 points.

CONNECT AND CHAT

Join the live Group Discussion for a real-time chat with all students, or start a private, one-on-one conversation using the Chat feature.

What is The Snooze Club?

The Snooze Club is an anonymous social platform where students can freely connect, share ideas, post feedback, and engage in both public and private discussions with their peers.

How does anonymity work on this platform?

Your identity is protected behind your chosen username. You can interact, post, and chat without revealing any personal information, ensuring all engagement is truly anonymous.

What are Credential Points?

Credential Points are a measure of your reputation within the community. Your points increase by +30 when your posts are highly upvoted and decrease by -30 when they are significantly downvoted, reflecting how the community values your contributions.

ü§ñ
How are posts and complaints handled?

All content is managed by the community. Posts and complaints are subject to user votes, which affect the author's credential points. For inappropriate content, users can use the report feature to flag it for review.

Can I chat with other students?

Yes. You can join the real-time Group Discussion to chat with everyone at once, or navigate to the Chat tab to start a private, one-on-one conversation with any other user on the platform.

Can I block another user?

Yes. If you no longer wish to interact with a specific person, you can block them from within the private chat window. This will prevent them from sending you any further messages.

ABOUT US

The Snooze Club is a dynamic space where student voices matter. Built on the principles of anonymity and community-driven moderation, we provide a platform where students can share anything from a casual thought to a serious complaint without hesitation.

With features like public group discussions, private one-on-one chats, and a unique credential points system based on community voting, The Snooze Club offers a truly interactive and engaging environment. Here, your contributions are valued, your identity is protected, and your conversations help shape the student experience.
